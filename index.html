About Us

Shri Balaji Global

House of Quality

We at Shri Balaji Global INC take pride and responsibility in being one of the largest millers and exporters of rice in the central India region. We have been into rice trade since 1953 and have established ourselves as one of the most esteemed and trusted rice brokers in central India under the banner name of Khandelwal Traders.

More

Our first manufacturing unit, Shri Balaji Rice Mill was established in the year 1993 and another facility Shreenath Rice Industries was set open in the Year 1999. Both units have an average production capacity of 8 Metric tonnes per hour.

More

Our Strength

Our procurement network is spread across the entire length & breadth of India. We have strong affiliations with over 5000 rice millers across India. We happen to be the leading supplier to almost every major rice exporter in India and have continued to provide quality and efficient services along with the ever changing market demands. Based on our trusted and transparent terms and efficient services we have developed strong business relationships with our clients over the decades and have emerged as leaders in the supply of quality rice.
